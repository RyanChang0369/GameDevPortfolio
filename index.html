Who Am I

I am a Senior majoring in Computer Science at MSU (Michigan State University), with a minor in Game Development. I have worked on a handful of school projects in game development, and I have a few unfinished personal projects that I used to learn the basics of Unity. In addition, I have developed a plethora of small scripts, mostly written in BASH, in my free time.

I specialize in programming, starting in early high school, with over 4 years experience specifically in C# and about 3 years experience in Unity. Through my experiences as a student, I have also gained proficiency in project management software, namely Trello, as well as source control software such as Git and Plastic SCM.

Check out my showcased projects or view my GitHub/itch.io pages in the contact section.
